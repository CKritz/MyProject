Welcome to the Binge!

In the high-paced world we live in, who has time to schedule TV viewing? The trend shows that 70% of Americans binge watch their favorite shows. Part of this trend is based on our scheduling practices, but it can also be attributed to show popularity.

In the past, we may have heard about a show that gained popularity during the third season, for example. Before the popularity of websites providing viewing of past episodes, viewers were at the mercy of paying for seasons on video or hoping for the availability at the local video stores or libraries. In this high technology age, accessibility to a large variety and TV shows and entertainment genres has made this an inconvenience no longer.

Please visit the Highlighted Genres to see a list of shows under our favorite categories and options where the series can be viewed.
